## Rule
Given the same inputs, the system must produce the same outputs.

## Applies To
- Acquisition
- Parsing
- Binding
- Queries
- Diagnostics

## Prohibited
- Hidden state
- Time-dependent logic
- Order-dependent side effects

## Rationale
Determinism is required for debugging, freezing, and trust.
